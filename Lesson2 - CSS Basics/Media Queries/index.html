Hello World!

We are the Google Developer Student Club at ENICarthage University, Tunisia Our goal is to create impact. Impact students and empower them to impact their communities through technology. What is GDSC? Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) is a program presented by Google Developers. GDSCs are university based community groups for students. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
